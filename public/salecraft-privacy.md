# salecraft Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-04-22. [TODO: legal review before GA]_

salecraft ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains
what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

## 1. Information We Collect

- **Account information** provided by Google OAuth: email, name, profile
  picture URL, Google account ID. We use this to create and authenticate
  your account.
- **Product usage data**: pages you visit, features you use, generated
  assets, credit consumption, and rough diagnostic metrics.
- **Device & browser data**: IP address, user-agent, approximate geolocation
  derived from IP, browser timezone.
- **Payment data**: processed by Stripe. We store only non-sensitive
  metadata (e.g., customer ID, subscription status); full card data never
  touches our servers.

## 2. How We Use Information

- Provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
- Authenticate users, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations.
- Send transactional emails (account notifications, receipts, security
  alerts). Marketing emails are opt-in and include an unsubscribe link.

## 3. Sharing & Processors

We do not sell your personal data. We share data with processors that help
us run the Service, including:

- **Google** (OAuth, Cloud Run hosting, Gemini model APIs)
- **Stripe** (payments)
- **Analytics providers** (product usage, anonymized where feasible)

Each processor is contractually bound to handle data consistent with this
Policy.

## 4. Shared Identity with Landing AI

salecraft and Landing AI share a single user directory so that you can use
one Google account across both products. Your account record is visible to
the authentication service; product-specific data (projects, assets,
conversations) is kept per-product and is not shared across products.

## 5. Retention

We retain account data while your account is active. If you delete your
account, we remove personal data within 30 days, except where we must
retain it to comply with law (e.g., tax, fraud prevention).

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct,
export, or delete your personal data. Email support@salecraft.ai to
exercise these rights.

## 7. Security

We use encryption in transit, access controls, and logging to protect your
data. No system is perfectly secure; report any suspected vulnerability to
support@salecraft.ai.

## 8. Changes

We may update this Policy. Material changes will be announced in-product or
by email. Continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance.

## 9. Contact

support@salecraft.ai
